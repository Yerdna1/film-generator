# Film Generator Pricing Guide

This document outlines the pricing structure for all AI providers and models supported by Film Generator, with a focus on KIE.ai's extensive model marketplace.

## KIE.ai Credit System

KIE.ai uses a credit-based pricing system:
- **1 credit = $0.005 USD**
- All prices shown include both credits and USD equivalent

## Complete Project Cost Estimation

For a typical 60-scene project:
- **Scenes**: 60 × $0.01 = $0.60 (Claude)
- **Characters**: 4 × $0.008 = $0.032 (Claude)
- **Images**: 64 × $0.10 = $6.40 (KIE Seedream)
- **Videos**: 60 × $0.10 = $6.00 (KIE Grok)
- **Voiceovers**: 240 lines × $0.025 = $6.00 (KIE ElevenLabs)
- **Music**: 1 × $0.05 = $0.05 (PiAPI)

**Total**: ~$19.08

## Notes

1. **KIE.ai Pricing**: All KIE models use credit-based pricing where 1 credit = $0.005 USD
2. **Character Counts**: TTS pricing is typically per 100 characters for easier estimation
3. **Video Duration**: Video models generate ~6 second clips
4. **Recommended Models**: Models marked with ⭐ offer the best balance of quality and cost
5. **Self-hosted Options**: Modal.com options require GPU infrastructure but offer lower per-unit costs
